What is Factify?

Do you often stumble upon digital media containing information that you know to be fictional, biased or misleading? News integrity in the media has seen a rapid decline in recent years. The 2016 US Presidential Elections highlighted the increasingly problematic issue of online bias and information integrity. The overwhelming volume of biased, misleading, or simply misinformed information published online can make it challenging to distinguish facts from "alternative" facts.

Now thereâ€™s something you can do about it. Factify is a Chrome extension that makes it easy to highlight untrustworthy online content, and annotate the source with the corrected information and references to more reputable, reliable sources. Factify aims to integrate with the media sources you use every day. It is accessible on the Chrome toolbar when you visit a news site, and is available as a Facebook plugin to link directly to the fact-verified source for an article.
