Why Factify?

The integrity of information published by the media has seen a rapid decline in recent years. The 2016 US Presidential Elections highlighted the increasingly problematic issue of information accuracy online. The overwhelming volume of biased, misleading, or simply misinformed news published online can make it challenging to distinguish facts from "alternative" facts. Do you often stumble upon digital media containing information that you know to be misleading, false, biased, or otherwise problematic? Now there is something you can do about it.

How can you help?

Factify enables users to highlight untrustworthy online content and annotate the source with the corrected information. Approved fact-verifications provide references to appropriate sources, ensuring the corrected information is reputable and verifiable.

Factify integrations.

Factify provides both a Chrome extension and a Facebook plugin to integrate crowd-sourced fact verification directly with the platforms and tools you already use. An option to toggle the Factify button appears on the Chrome toolbar when you visit any Factify-supported news or media sites. Toggling the Factify button highlights the article in focus with verified Factify annotations, enabling you to discern fact and fiction. The Facebook plugin adds a Factify button to all shared news articles, linking directly to the Factify-ed article.
